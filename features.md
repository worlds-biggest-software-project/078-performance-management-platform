## Feature Analysis by Solution

### Lattice

**Core features**
- Configurable performance review cycles (annual, semi-annual, quarterly, project-based) with customisable question banks and rating scales
- 360-degree feedback collection from peers, direct reports, and managers with configurable anonymity thresholds
- OKR and goal management with company-to-team-to-individual cascade, progress check-in prompts, and direct linkage into review cycles
- Calibration workflow enabling HR and senior leadership to compare ratings across teams, adjust for grade inflation, and ensure cross-departmental consistency
- Compensation integration allowing merit cycle decisions to be made directly within performance review data context

**Differentiating features**
- AI-generated performance review narratives: Lattice AI summarises an employee's goal completion, continuous feedback, and check-in history into a draft review narrative, reducing manager writing time by an estimated 70–80%
- AI-driven calibration summaries that surface statistical distribution anomalies and outlier managers during calibration sessions
- Structured improvement plans generated by AI based on performance signals
- All-in-one positioning covering OKRs, reviews, engagement, and HRIS data in a single system of record

**UX patterns**
- Manager dashboard consolidating direct reports' goal progress, pending reviews, and upcoming 1-on-1 agendas on one screen
- Inline continuous feedback widget that employees can send and receive at any time without leaving the tool
- Review wizard guiding managers step-by-step through draft, calibration, and publish stages

**Integration points**
- HRIS sync with BambooHR, Workday, ADP, Rippling, and Gusto
- Slack and Microsoft Teams for nudges, feedback requests, and review deadline reminders
- Compensation platforms (Mercer, Radford) for benchmarking data in merit cycles

**Known gaps**
- Engagement survey science less rigorous than Culture Amp's purpose-built methodology
- OKR depth and enterprise cascade management less mature than Betterworks for 500+ employee organisations
- Benchmarking data for compensation relies on third-party integrations rather than native market data
- AI review generation quality depends heavily on the quantity of continuous feedback logged in the system

**Licence / IP notes**
- Proprietary SaaS; all performance data stored in Lattice cloud
- AI review generation is a closed model; no published methodology for bias detection in generated narratives
- EU AI Act high-risk classification applies to AI-assisted performance scoring in EU deployments

### 15Five

**Core features**
- Weekly check-in workflow: employees submit a short structured update covering wins, blockers, and morale; managers receive a digest with response prompts
- OKR management with individual, team, and company-level goal alignment
- 360 peer review and upward feedback collection integrated with review cycles
- HR Outcomes Dashboard aggregating engagement, performance, and retention signals into a single executive view
- 1-on-1 meeting agenda builder with shared notes and action item tracking

**Differentiating features**
- Manager coaching AI: generates personalised coaching recommendations based on the specific team dynamics, engagement score, and performance signals visible for each manager's direct reports
- High five peer recognition integrated directly into the weekly check-in flow
- Engage module combines pulse survey data with performance signals to generate predictive manager effectiveness scores

**UX patterns**
- Lightweight weekly ritual design — the check-in takes under five minutes and is the primary data collection mechanism
- Mobile-first experience making the tool accessible to managers who are rarely at a desk

**Integration points**
- BambooHR, Gusto, Namely, Rippling for HRIS roster sync
- Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Google Workspace

**Known gaps**
- OKR cascade and alignment management less sophisticated than Betterworks for large enterprises
- Calibration tooling less mature than Lattice
- Benchmarking data for contextualising engagement and performance scores is smaller than Culture Amp's

**Licence / IP notes**
- Proprietary SaaS; no open-source components
- Manager coaching AI methodology is not publicly documented

### Betterworks

**Core features**
- Enterprise OKR management with multi-level cascading, cross-team alignment mapping, and programme-level OKR analytics
- Continuous performance management replacing annual reviews with quarterly check-ins, structured conversations, and ongoing coaching
- Performance review workflows with configurable cycles, multi-rater collection, and calibration tooling
- Executive-grade OKR reporting showing company strategy execution health across business units

**Differentiating features**
- Deep OKR-to-review linkage: objective progress data is surfaced directly inside review forms so managers review actual outcomes, not impressions
- Strong calibration workflow with statistical distribution views across departments, flagging systematic rating inflation or deflation
- Purpose-built for organisations with 500+ employees where OKR cascade complexity makes self-service tools unworkable

**UX patterns**
- OKR alignment map providing a visual tree showing how every team and individual goal connects to company-level objectives
- Manager conversation guides providing structured prompts for regular coaching conversations

**Integration points**
- Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, and Oracle HCM native connectors for enterprise HRIS
- Slack and Microsoft Teams

**Known gaps**
- Not suitable for SMBs; minimum 500-employee requirement excludes most of the market
- UI is functional but less polished than Lattice or Leapsome
- No native engagement survey module; relies on integrations

**Licence / IP notes**
- Proprietary SaaS; no open-source components

## Cross-Cutting Feature Themes

### Table-Stakes Features
- Configurable performance review cycles with self-assessment, manager review, and optional peer feedback
- OKR or goal management with individual-to-company alignment visibility
- 360-degree feedback collection with configurable anonymity thresholds
- Continuous feedback mechanism (praise, coaching, and development notes outside formal review cycles)
- Calibration workflow for cross-team rating normalisation
- HRIS roster sync for automated employee lifecycle management
- Role-based access control (employee, manager, HR admin, executive views)
- SOC 2 Type II certification for enterprise data security requirements

### Differentiating Features
- AI-generated review narrative drafts grounded in goal completion and feedback history
- Real-time bias detection in peer feedback language before submission
- Predictive manager effectiveness scoring from multi-signal behavioural data
- Competency framework management linked to role definitions and career ladders
- Calibration statistical analytics flagging systematic rating outlier managers
- Compensation integration enabling merit decisions in performance review context
- Learning path recommendations triggered automatically by performance gaps

### Underserved Areas / Opportunities
- No credible open-source performance management platform exists in 2026; the entire market is proprietary SaaS
- AI review generation that is demonstrably bias-tested and transparent in methodology
- Cascade OKR management for organisations below 500 employees at sub-$5/ee/mo pricing
- Performance analytics for frontline and deskless workers outside the knowledge-worker paradigm
- Works council-compliant performance management in Germany, France, and the Netherlands (requiring co-determination consultation before AI-assisted scoring deployment)

### AI-Augmentation Candidates
- Evidence-grounded review narrative generation synthesising OKR data, peer feedback, and project outcomes
- Automated bias flagging in peer feedback language (gender-coded words, attribution errors) before submission
- OKR cascade validation that identifies misaligned, duplicate, or conflicting objectives across teams
- Predictive calibration pre-fill based on objective performance signals and historical manager rating patterns
- Attrition risk scoring from combined performance trajectory, engagement trend, and compensation gap signals

## Legal & IP Summary

- All leading performance management platforms are proprietary SaaS; no open-source alternatives of commercial quality exist
- EU AI Act (effective 2026) classifies AI tools used in employment decisions as "high-risk"; performance scoring and review generation AI in EU deployments requires transparency documentation, conformity assessment, and human oversight mechanisms
- GDPR Article 22 prohibits solely automated employment decisions; any AI-generated performance rating must include a human review step with meaningful override capability
- 360-degree feedback data is personal data under GDPR; anonymity thresholds must be technically enforced, not just policy-stated
- OKR frameworks (Intel/Google methodology) are not patented; freely implementable
- Balanced Scorecard is also not patented; freely implementable with attribution required only in academic contexts
- No patent-encumbered techniques identified in standard performance review workflows; AI narrative generation techniques may be subject to broad LLM-related patents held by OpenAI, Google, or Microsoft (currently unenforced in HR SaaS context)
- SOC 2 Type II is a practical prerequisite for enterprise sales; requires annual third-party audit

## Recommended Feature Scope

**Must-have (MVP)**
- Configurable performance review cycles with self, manager, and peer review forms
- OKR management with company-to-team-to-individual cascade and progress tracking
- Continuous feedback module for peer and manager recognition and coaching notes
- Calibration workflow with rating distribution view and adjustment capability
- HRIS roster sync with BambooHR, Workday, and Rippling at minimum
- Role-based access with distinct employee, manager, HR admin, and executive permission tiers

**Should-have (v1.1)**
- AI-generated review narrative drafts grounded in goals, feedback, and check-in data
- Real-time bias-language detection in peer feedback submissions (flag before save)
- Manager effectiveness dashboard aggregating direct report performance, engagement, and check-in completion signals
- Competency framework builder linked to role definitions and career progression paths
- Compensation integration allowing merit increase inputs during review workflow
- SOC 2 Type II certification and GDPR data processing agreement

**Nice-to-have (backlog)**
- Predictive attrition risk scoring combining performance trajectory and engagement signals
- OKR alignment conflict detection with AI-suggested corrections
- Learning path recommendations automatically surfaced from performance gap data
- Succession planning module identifying high-potential employees from performance and development data
- Works council compliance configuration for EU co-determination requirements
